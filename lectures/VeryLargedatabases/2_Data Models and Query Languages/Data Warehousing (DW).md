A central system that consolidates vast amounts of historical and current data from various sources (like sales, marketing, operations) into one place, transforming and cleaning it for analysis, reporting, and [[Business Intelligence (BI)]] to support strategic decision-making

- An enterprise may have dozens/hundreds of [[Online Transaction Processing (OLTP)]] systems
- Most of these are operated by separate teams
- A [[Data Warehousing (DW)]] integrates such for analytic purposes
